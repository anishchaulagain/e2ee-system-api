# ADR-000: Architecture Decision Record Template

## Status

PROPOSED | ACCEPTED | DEPRECATED | SUPERSEDED BY [ADR-XXX]

## Context

What is the issue? What forces are at play? What technical or business constraints exist?

## Decision

What is the change that we're making? State it clearly and concisely.

## Consequences

### Positive

- What becomes easier or better?

### Negative

- What becomes harder or worse?
- What technical debt does this introduce?

### Risks

- What could go wrong?

## Alternatives considered

List other options you evaluated and why they were rejected.

---

**Usage:** Copy this file as `docs/decisions/NNN-title.md` for each architectural decision.
Record decisions when: choosing a framework, designing a data model, picking a deployment strategy,
introducing a new pattern, or making any choice that future developers will ask "why did we do this?"
